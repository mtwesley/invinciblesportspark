President Weah Dedicates Invincible Sports Park

P .Vangerline Kpotoe & Michlyne N. Williams

April 11, 2022

Youth - Sports - Development

MONROVIA, (LINA) - President George M. Weah is expected to officially dedicate the newly constructed Invincible Park on Friday, April 15, 2022, the Minister of State Without Museum Trokon Kpui has disclosed.

Min. Kpui said the park is not only a football facility, but also consists of a children's playground, housing units, lounge (VIP) and lots of goodies for the public.

He pointed out that approximately 20 international soccer legends and representatives from FIFA are expected to grace the occasion.

He named former AC Milan player Marco Simone as well as retired African soccer stars Diedier Drogba, Samuel Etoâ€™o and Mohammed Kallon as those expected to grace the occasion.

He noted that the President thought it necessary to construct housing units along with the park for residents, stating that NPA, NASCORP and the Liberia Maritime Authority are the institutions funding the project.

It can be recalled that On Tuesday 9 February 2021, Pres. Weah broke ground for the construction of a new sports park dubbed Invisible Sports Park.

The Invincible Sports Park was initiated to replace - the famous Invincible Eleven Majesty Sports club known as I.E. Practice ground, near the James Payne Spriggs Field, Airfield Sinkor, as a way of developing the nation through sports.

Speaking about maintenance, Min. Kpui stated that the park will be heavily guided by authorities to ensure proper hygienic and decency, adding that, anyone who will be caught messing up the facility or destroying public properties in the park will face the full weight of the law.

President Weah over the weekend toured the park ahead of the dedication and official opening to the Public. During the tour the Liberian Leader called on Liberians to gather and enjoy the beauty of the place.
