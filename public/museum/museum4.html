"He gave me a chance... and I made use of that opportunity"

Under Wenger, Weah grew to be a prolific striker, working outside the penalty area, confusing the minds and bodies of helpless defenders and attacking with the highest level of pace, stamina, and technical ability sport had ever seen. His seasons with Monaco, were marked with a number of accomplishments, having won the Coupe de France in 1991 and African Footballer of the Year in 1989. Weah credits Wenger as an important influence in his life and career and they remain the best of friends.
